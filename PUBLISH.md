## Description (markdown — colle telle quelle sur les deux)

```markdown
# EMI Enchant Info

Browse every enchantment in the game from the **EMI** recipe screen, with all the
details you actually want to know — at a glance.

Search any enchantment (or click the enchanted book category) and instantly see:

- 📖 **Every level** as an enchanted book (1 → max)
- ⚔️ **Applicable items** — what the enchantment can go on
- 🚫 **Conflicting enchantments** — what it can't be combined with
- ⚖️ **Weight** and per-level **enchanting cost** ranges
- 🪧 Whether it shows up in the **enchanting table**, from **villagers**, in **loot**,
  and whether it's a **treasure** enchantment or a **curse**
- 📝 The enchantment **description** (when provided by the mod/pack)

Works with **modded enchantments** too — anything registered in the game shows up
automatically.

## Requirements
- **NeoForge 1.21.1**
- **[EMI](https://modrinth.com/mod/emi)** (required, client-side)
